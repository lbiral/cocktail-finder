Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • whiskey
  • sugar
  • bitters
  • orange
  • cherry

Recipe

In a glass, muddle 1 sugar cube with 2 dashes of bitters and a splash of water. Add 2 oz of whiskey and stir. Add ice and stir again. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.
