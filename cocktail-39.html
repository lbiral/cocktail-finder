Martinez

Ingredients

  • gin
  • sweet vermouth
  • maraschino liqueur
  • orange bitters

Recipe

In a mixing glass, combine 1.5 oz gin, 1.5 oz sweet vermouth, 0.25 oz maraschino liqueur, and 2 dashes of orange bitters. Stir well and strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with an orange twist.
