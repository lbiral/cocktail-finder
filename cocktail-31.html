Long Island Iced Tea

Ingredients

  • vodka
  • tequila
  • rum
  • gin
  • orange liqueur
  • lemon
  • cola

Recipe

In a shaker, combine 0.5 oz vodka, 0.5 oz tequila, 0.5 oz rum, 0.5 oz gin, 0.5 oz orange liqueur, and 0.75 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice. Top with a splash of cola and garnish with a lemon wheel.
