Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • whiskey
  • lemon
  • simple syrup

Recipe

In a shaker, combine 2 oz whiskey, 0.75 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice, and 0.5 oz simple syrup. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice. Garnish with a lemon wheel and a maraschino cherry.
