Trinidad Sour

Ingredients

  • angostura bitters
  • orgeat
  • lemon
  • rye whiskey

Recipe

In a shaker, combine 1 oz Angostura bitters, 1 oz orgeat syrup, 0.75 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice, and 0.5 oz rye whiskey. Shake well and strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lemon twist.
