Casino

Ingredients

  • gin
  • maraschino liqueur
  • orange bitters
  • lemon

Recipe

In a shaker, combine 2 oz gin, 0.25 oz maraschino liqueur, 2 dashes of orange bitters, and 0.5 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice. Shake well and strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lemon twist and a maraschino cherry.
