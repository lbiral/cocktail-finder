Mai Tai

Ingredients

  • light rum
  • dark rum
  • orange curacao
  • orgeat
  • lime

Recipe

In a shaker, combine 1 oz light rum, 1 oz dark rum, 0.5 oz orange curacao, 0.5 oz orgeat syrup, and 1 oz freshly squeezed lime juice. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with crushed ice. Garnish with a lime wheel, a sprig of mint, and a maraschino cherry.
