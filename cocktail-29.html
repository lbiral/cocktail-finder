Planter's Punch

Ingredients

  • dark rum
  • orange juice
  • pineapple juice
  • grenadine
  • lime
  • bitters

Recipe

In a shaker, combine 2 oz dark rum, 2 oz orange juice, 2 oz pineapple juice, 0.5 oz freshly squeezed lime juice, 0.5 oz grenadine, and 2 dashes of bitters. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice. Garnish with an orange wheel and a maraschino cherry.
