Sex on the Beach

Ingredients

  • vodka
  • peach schnapps
  • orange juice
  • cranberry juice

Recipe

In a shaker, combine 1.5 oz vodka, 0.75 oz peach schnapps, 2 oz orange juice, and 2 oz cranberry juice. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice. Garnish with an orange wheel and a maraschino cherry.
