French 75

Ingredients

  • gin
  • lemon
  • simple syrup
  • champagne

Recipe

In a shaker, combine 1.5 oz gin, 0.5 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice, and 0.5 oz simple syrup. Shake well and strain into a chilled champagne flute. Top with 2 oz champagne and garnish with a lemon twist.
