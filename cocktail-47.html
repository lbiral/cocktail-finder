Old Cuban

Ingredients

  • rum
  • mint
  • lime
  • simple syrup
  • bitters
  • champagne

Recipe

In a shaker, muddle 6-8 mint leaves with 0.75 oz freshly squeezed lime juice and 0.5 oz simple syrup. Add 1.5 oz rum and 2 dashes of bitters. Shake well and strain into a chilled coupe glass. Top with 2 oz champagne and garnish with a mint sprig.
